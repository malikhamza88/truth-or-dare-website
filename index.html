Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Truth or Dare Game ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Game Preferences: Your chosen game settings including AI themes, tones, and player group preferences
  • Notification Preferences: Your choice to receive game reminders and achievement notifications

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how you use the app, including game statistics, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system version, and app crash reports for debugging purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: To provide personalized Truth or Dare questions and remember your preferences
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app's performance and user experience
  • Notifications: To send you game reminders and achievement celebrations (only if you opt-in)
  • AI Features: To generate personalized questions through Google's Gemini AI service

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with:

  • Analytics Services: Mixpanel for app usage analytics
  • AI Services: Google Gemini AI for question generation (no personal data shared)
  • Subscription Services: RevenueCat for managing premium subscriptions

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Data Storage: We do not store personal identifying information on our servers
  • Secure Transmission: All network communications use industry-standard encryption

Your Rights and Choices

  • Notification Control: You can disable notifications in your device settings
  • Data Access: Your game data is stored locally and accessible only to you
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Premium Features: Purchase premium subscription to remove ads and access AI features

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Mixpanel: For analytics (Mixpanel Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini AI: For AI question generation (Google Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All content is family-friendly and appropriate for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or our website.

Permissions Explained

  • Notifications: To send you personalized game reminders, celebrate your achievements, and provide seasonal notifications to enhance your gaming experience

This permission is optional and the app functions fully without granting it.
